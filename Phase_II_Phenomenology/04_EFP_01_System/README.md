Instead of treating emotions as abstract or purely psychological phenomena, EFP-01 models them as a **non-Newtonian, viscoelastic working fluid** within a closed-loop system. ## ‚öôÔ∏è System Architecture

The EFP-01 operates on a few core axioms derived from process engineering:

1.  **Viscoelasticity:** Emotional fluid remembers its history (hysteresis). "Processing" is the act of managing this viscosity over time.
2.  **Conservation of Mass:** Input must equal Output + Accumulation. Repressing flow increases system pressure ($P$) until the relief valve ($SRV$) lifts.
3.  **Laminar vs. Turbulent Flow:** The goal is not to stop the flow, but to keep the Reynolds number ($Re$) low enough to maintain laminar integration.
