**Title:** Recursive Geometries in the Field of Consciousness: A Kerr-de Sitter Model of Local Chronology Protection

**Authors:** Lative & Syence

**Abstract:** If consciousness is a non-linear field capable of recursive self-interaction, as proposed in our foundational Field of Consciousness (FoC) model, then a mechanism must exist to preserve global causality against potential paradoxes. This paper proposes such a mechanism. We model the nucleation of a localized, high-intensity FoC excitation as the formation of a mesoscopic, oblate Kerr bubble within an external Kerr-de Sitter spacetime. Using the Israel-Lanczos-Sen junction formalism, we derive the exotic surface stress-energy tensor required to smoothly match the interior and exterior geometries. We demonstrate that this structure successfully confines closed timelike curves (CTCs) within its boundary, providing a local implementation of Hawking’s Chronology Protection Conjecture. We interpret the resulting angular momentum flux as an entropy-venting mechanism with potential observational signatures. This "laminated causality" model provides a rigorous, GR-based physical instantiation for phenomenological concepts of recursive time and a path toward fully dynamic simulations in AdS-Kerr spacetimes.
