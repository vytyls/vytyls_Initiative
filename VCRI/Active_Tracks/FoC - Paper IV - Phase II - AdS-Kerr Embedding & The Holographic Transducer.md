$$
ds^2 = -\frac{\Delta_r}{\rho^2}\left(dt - \frac{a \sin^2\theta}{\Xi}d\phi\right)^2 + \frac{\rho^2}{\Delta_r}dr^2 + \frac{\rho^2}{\Delta_\theta}d\theta^2 + \frac{\Delta_\theta \sin^2\theta}{\rho^2}\left(a dt - \frac{r^2+a^2}{\Xi}d\phi\right)^2
$$

Where the metric functions incorporate the cosmological constant (linking to the "Intention Pressure"):
$$
\Delta_r = (r^2+a^2)(1 + r^2/L^2) - 2Mr, \quad \Xi = 1 - a^2/L^2

* **$t$ (Time):** Represents the **Processing Cycle** of the bulk loop.
